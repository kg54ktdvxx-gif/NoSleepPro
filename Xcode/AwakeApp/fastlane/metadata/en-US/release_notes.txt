Initial release of No Sleep Pro — a powerful menu bar utility to keep your Mac awake.

• Timer presets: 15 min to indefinite
• Smart automation: app triggers, schedules, hardware detection
• Closed-lid mode for external display setups
• Mouse jiggler to prevent Away status
• Battery protection with configurable threshold
• Global keyboard shortcut (⌘⇧A)
• 8 menu bar icon styles
• Zero data collection
