# Privacy Policy

**No Sleep Pro**
Last updated: February 7, 2026

## Overview

No Sleep Pro is a macOS menu bar utility that prevents your Mac from sleeping. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**No Sleep Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

## Permissions

The app may request the following system permissions:

### Accessibility (Optional)
Required only if you enable:
- **Mouse Jiggler**: Moves your cursor slightly to prevent "Away" status
- **Global Keyboard Shortcut**: Detects key presses to toggle sleep prevention

This permission is used solely for these features. No data is collected or transmitted.

### Notifications (Optional)
Used to notify you when:
- A timer ends
- Battery protection activates

All notifications are processed locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services

No Sleep Pro does not use:
- Analytics or tracking
- Crash reporting services
- Advertising networks
- Any third-party services

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally:
- **Settings**: Stored in macOS UserDefaults
- **No cloud sync**: Your preferences stay on your device
- **No accounts**: No sign-up or login required

## Children's Privacy

No Sleep Pro does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
