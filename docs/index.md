# No Sleep Pro

**Keep Your Mac Awake**

No Sleep Pro is a lightweight macOS menu bar app that prevents your Mac from sleeping with a single click.

## Features

- **One-Click Activation** - Toggle sleep prevention from your menu bar
- **Timer Presets** - 15 min, 30 min, 1 hour, 2 hours, 4 hours, or indefinitely
- **Battery Protection** - Auto-stops at configurable battery level
- **Schedules** - Activate during work hours automatically
- **App Triggers** - Activate when specific apps are running
- **Hardware Triggers** - Activate when power or external display connected
- **Mouse Jiggler** - Prevent "Away" status in messaging apps
- **Keyboard Shortcut** - Quick toggle with global hotkey
- **8 Icon Styles** - Choose your preferred menu bar look
